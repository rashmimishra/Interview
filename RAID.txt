                                RAID(Redundant Array of Independent Disks)
							 Reliability,Availability,Performance and Capacity

RAID 0 consists of stripping without parity or mirroring.

RAID 1 consists of mirroring, without parity or stripping. 

RAID 2 consists of bit level stripping and dedicated Hamming code parity.

RAID 3 consists of byte level stripping and dedicated parity.

RAID 4 consists of block level stripping and dedicated parity.

RAID 5 consists of block level stripping and distributed parity.

RAID 6 consists of block level stripping and double distributed parity.
